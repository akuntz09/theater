ACTOR 3: i've made this recipe and it is the easiest way i've found. no thermometer required: microwave hard candy recipe 1 cup granulated sugar 1/2 cup light corn syrup 1/4 tsp. candy flavor oil powdered sugar -- (optional) 1. thoroughly mix sugar and corn syrup in microwave safe glass bowl 2. cover with plastic wrap 3. microwave on high for 3 minutes and 15 seconds * 4. quickly stir the sugar mixture and then cover with a new sheet of plastic wrap 5. microwave on high for 3 minutes and 15 seconds * 6. after boiling has ceased, add coloring and flavoring 7. pour syrup quickly into mold or pan * please note: all microwaves are not created equal. this recipe is designed to work in a standard household microwave with a power rating between 600-700 watts. mini-microwave and/or commercial microwaves are not recommended.
